Why DFC

What separates a licensed general contractor from a handyman with a business card.

Virginia Class A contractors can legally perform any construction, remodeling or repair work of unlimited dollar value. Class B is limited to $120,000 per project. Class C is limited to $10,000. When you're remodeling a kitchen or adding on to your home, you want a Class A license — that's who DFC is.

Class A also means we carry the required general liability and workers' compensation insurance for a company of our size, and that we've passed the Virginia DPOR licensing exam covering business law, project management and trade standards. That matters when someone is working in your home and something unexpected comes up.

We've been working in Falls Church and Northern Virginia long enough to know how the county building departments operate, which structural conditions to expect in which neighborhoods, and what realistic project timelines look like in this market. We don't overpromise on schedule to win the job and underdeliver once you're committed.
